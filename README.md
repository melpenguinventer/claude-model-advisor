# AI Model Advisor

An interactive tool to compare and choose the right AI model for your task. Compare Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and more to find the perfect fit for your needs.

## Features
- **Smart recommendations** across multiple AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google)
- **Cost-benefit analysis** to ensure you're getting value for your money
- **Auto-generated prompts** optimized for your chosen model
- **Complexity helper** for when you're unsure about task difficulty
- **Multi-model comparison** including vision/multimodal capabilities

## Models Covered

### Anthropic (Claude)
- **Claude Haiku 4.5** - Fast and affordable
- **Claude Sonnet 4.6** - Balanced workhorse  
- **Claude Opus 4.6** - Deep reasoning for complex problems

### OpenAI (ChatGPT)
- **GPT-4o** - Advanced reasoning with vision capabilities
- **GPT-4 Turbo** - Cost-effective reasoning alternative

### Google (Gemini)
- **Gemini Pro 2.0** - Fast multimodal with long context

## How to Use
1. Visit the link below
2. Answer questions about your task (complexity, speed, budget, etc.)
3. Get a personalized model recommendation with cost breakdown
4. Copy the generated prompt optimized for your chosen model
5. Paste into your preferred AI tool and start working
